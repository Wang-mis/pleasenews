Swiss government approves draft law to ban Hamas
REUTERS
SEPTEMBER 4, 2024 15:50
20240904
https://www.jpost.com/breaking-news/article-818713
The Swiss government approved a draft law on Wednesday to ban Hamas, which it considers to be a "terrorist organization," and said anyone who violates the ban would be punished with a prison sentence or fine.Under the new law, which must go to parliament, Hamas and successor groups, as well as organizations and groups that act on behalf of or in the name of Hamas, will be banned.

IDF jets strike Hezbollah targets on the southern Lebanon border
JERUSALEM POST STAFF
SEPTEMBER 2, 2024 18:11
20240902
https://www.jpost.com/breaking-news/article-817431
IAF fighter jets attacked a Hezbollah military structure near Markaba in southern Lebanon, right on the border with Israel, the IDF announced on Monday.About 30 rockets were detected crossing into Israel from the territory of Lebanon earlier in the day; there were no reported casualties.

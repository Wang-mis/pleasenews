US National Security Adviser Jake Sullivan will hold a virtual meeting on Sunday with families of American hostages held by Hamas, a US official said.This meeting comes after American hostage Hersh Golberg-Polin's lifeless body was located by the IDF in a Hamas tunnel in the Rafah area early Sunday morning.

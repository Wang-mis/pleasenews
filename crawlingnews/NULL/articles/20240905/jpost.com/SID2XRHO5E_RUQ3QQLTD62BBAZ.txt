Police arrest three suspects for murder of the two Eritreans in Tel Aviv nearly two weeks ago
JERUSALEM POST STAFF
SEPTEMBER 3, 2024 18:02
20240903
https://www.jpost.com/breaking-news/article-817587
The police arrested three foreign nationals from Eritrea, in their 30s from Tel Aviv, on suspicion of involvement in the murder of two Eritreans in a fight in the south of the city about a week and a half ago, according to Israeli media.The police will ask the court to extend their detention tomorrow.

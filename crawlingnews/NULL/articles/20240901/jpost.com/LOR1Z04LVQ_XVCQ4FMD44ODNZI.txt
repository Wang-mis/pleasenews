Mossad Chief David Barnea met with Qatari PM on a hostage deal in Doha
TOVAH LAZAROFF
SEPTEMBER 2, 2024 21:02
20240903
https://www.jpost.com/breaking-news/article-817455
Mossad Chief David Barnea met with Qatari Prime Minister Mohammed al-Thani in Doha on Monday to discuss ways to advance an agreement to secure the release of the remaining 101 hostages, a source confirmed to The Jerusalem Post.The meeting took place as US President Joe Biden met in Washington with his negotiating team to seek a way to advance the deal, saying “We're not giving up. We're going to continue to push as hard as we can.”

Islamic State claims responsibility for deadly Kabul blast
REUTERS
SEPTEMBER 3, 2024 14:51
20240903
https://www.jpost.com/breaking-news/article-817553
Islamic State claimed responsibility on Tuesday for a deadly explosion in Afghanistan's capital, Kabul, a day earlier.In a post on its Telegram account, the militant group said one of its members detonated an explosive vest, killing and injuring more than 45 people.  On Monday, Kabul police spokesman Khalid Zadran said the blast killed six people and wounded 13 others.

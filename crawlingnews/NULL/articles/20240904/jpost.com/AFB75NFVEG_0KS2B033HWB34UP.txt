Mother of murdered hostage Ori Danino says she recently received sign of life
JERUSALEM POST STAFF
SEPTEMBER 2, 2024 10:49
20240902
https://www.jpost.com/breaking-news/article-817350
Einav Danino, the mother of murdered hostage Ori Danino, whose body was recovered by the IDF from Gaza on Saturday, told Kan Reshet Bet on Monday that she received a sign of life from her son three weeks ago.The IDF said that the Danino, along with five other hostages, had been executed by Hamas shortly before the military was able to get to them.
